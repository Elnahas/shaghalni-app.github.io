  • 'Employer': Individuals or entities posting job opportunities.

Eligibility

Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account, post jobs, or apply for positions via the "Shaghalni" application.

Account Creation

To access our services, users must create an account. By creating an account, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate, current, and complete information.
  • Keep your account details updated.
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations while using our services.

During account creation, you may be asked to provide:

  • Email address: We collect your email address to send account-related notifications and updates.
  • Password: You will be required to create a secure password for accessing your account.
  • Google Sign-In: Alternatively, you may choose to sign in using your Google account for quicker access.
  • Facebook Login: You can also use your Facebook account for convenient access.

Data Collection

We collect and process the following information to improve our services and user experience:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Profile picture
  • Job preferences and application history
  • City of residence
  • Date of birth
  • Gender (Male/Female)
  • Email address
  • Password (hashed and securely stored)
  • Location data: Collected when searching for nearby jobs or posting a job to record its location for better matching.

Services Section

The "Shaghalni" application allows users to list one service per account. To add a service, users may provide the following optional details:

  • Service Name (Required)
  • Specialization (Required)
  • Cover Image (Required)
  • Profile Picture (Required)
  • Service Address (Required)
  • Phone Number (Required)
  • WhatsApp Number (Optional)
  • Facebook Link (Optional)
  • Instagram Link (Optional)
  • YouTube Link (Optional)
  • National ID (Front & Back) (Optional, for verification purposes)

Service verification is not mandatory, but we encourage users to submit the necessary documents to enhance trust among users.

The listing of services is completely free, and "Shaghalni" does not charge any fees for service providers.
